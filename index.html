Who are we?

We believe in souls. We believe in soul mates. Our photos aim to capture people at their best and their worst—when there’s nothing left to be said and it’s all out on the table.

The Irish Goodbye means “there’s no hard feelings.” It’s not “goodbye”, it’s ”see you later.” It’s a reminder that there’s no rush—we’re all on the same ride, wherever it might be going. Slow down, look around, and be grateful.

Stay out of the deep end,
Nazir Agah & Avand Amiri
