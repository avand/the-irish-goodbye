The Irish Goodbye

We believe in souls. And we believe in soul mates. Our photos aim to capture people at their best and their worst - when then there's nothing left to be said and it's all out on the table. Our perfect shot isn't about lighting or poses. It's all about you. And we want to get to know you so that we can capture moments you'll cherish forever.

When we shoot your event, you'll receive ~300 shots in high resolution, minimally retouched. We'll also leave you with a package of ~30 unique prints. And we may use some of our favorite shots for our website.

Our signature is candid photos that capture emotion - not poses. We'll snap shots of the wedding party, family, and friends as situations present themselves. We don't like to arrange situations.
